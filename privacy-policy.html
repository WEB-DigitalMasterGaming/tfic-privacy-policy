Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the TFIC Event Companion mobile application (package name: com.tfic.tficmobileapp), which is published on Google Play under the official developer account, DigitalMaster Entertainment.

DigitalMaster Entertainment, in partnership with The Frontier Initiative Corporation (TFIC), is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TFIC Event Companion app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register for membership or contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and other details necessary for account setup.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about your interactions with the app, including device type and feature usage, to improve our services.
  • Event Data: When you RSVP or interact with events, we may store related information to provide calendar features.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your membership account.
  • To provide event calendar features, including RSVPs and notifications within the TFIC Event Companion app.
  • To send you updates about your account, including login information and changes to our policies.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. When your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

5. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. We may share data with trusted providers who help us operate our services. Security measures are in place to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections if your data is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Withdraw consent for specific processing activities.

To exercise your rights, contact us at administration@tficorg.org.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at administration@tficorg.org.
